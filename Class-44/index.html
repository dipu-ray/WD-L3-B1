A Legacy of Learning
and Leadership

Founded in 1979, Greenwood Academy has been a beacon of quality education. Our holistic approach blends rigorous academics with arts, sports, and character development.

  • Nationally accredited curriculum with IB & Cambridge pathways
  • Over 120 trained and certified faculty members
  • State-of-the-art STEM labs, libraries, and sports facilities
  • Dedicated student counseling and wellbeing programs
  • Active parent-teacher partnership and community events
