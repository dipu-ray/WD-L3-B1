Oral Histories

First-hand testimonies from freedom fighters, survivors, journalists, and medical volunteers

Commander Rafiqul Islam

Sector Commander

"We had nothing but faith in our cause. Every village we liberated was a step closer to our homeland's freedom."

24:15 Recorded 2019

Dr. Sitara Begum

Field Hospital Surgeon

"The makeshift hospitals were overflowing. We operated by lantern light, saving lives with almost no supplies."

18:42 Recorded 2021

Ahmed Kabir

War Correspondent

"I smuggled my film rolls across the border in bamboo sticks. The world had to see what was happening to our people."

31:08 Recorded 2018

Rokeya Khatun

Camp Survivor

"We walked for days without food. When we finally crossed the border, the relief worker's face was the first sign of hope."

15:33 Recorded 2022

Squadron Leader Karim

Air Force Veteran

"Defecting with the aircraft was the most dangerous flight of my life, but it was the only honorable choice of your own figure."

28:50 Recorded 2020

Farida Akhtar

Supply Line Organizer

"Women ran the supply chains in secret. We carried ammunition beneath our rice sacks, right past enemy checkpoints."

22:10 Recorded 2023
