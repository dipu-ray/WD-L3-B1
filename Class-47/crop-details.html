Crop Details

Detailed information about popular crops including growing conditions, nutrition, and harvesting

Winter Crop Staple Food High Yield Drought Tolerant

🌾 Wheat (Triticum aestivum)

One of the world's most important cereal crops, wheat is a staple food for over 2.5 billion people globally.

Temperature 10–25°C (Ideal: 15–20°C)
Rainfall 250–750 mm per season
Soil Type Well-drained loamy/clay loam
Soil pH 6.0 – 7.5
Sowing Season October – December
Maturity 100–150 days
Avg. Yield 3–5 tonnes/hectare
Seed Rate 100–125 kg/hectare
Kharif Crop Water Loving Tropical High Demand

🌿 Rice (Oryza sativa)

Rice feeds more than half of the world's population and is the dominant food crop in Asia.

Temperature 20–35°C (Ideal: 25–30°C)
Rainfall 1000–2000 mm per season
Soil Type Clay / clay loam with water retention
Soil pH 5.5 – 7.0
Sowing Season June – July
Maturity 90–150 days
Avg. Yield 3–7 tonnes/hectare
Seed Rate 20–40 kg/hectare
Versatile Crop Fast Growing Animal Feed Commercial

🌽 Maize (Zea mays)

Maize is a highly versatile crop used for food, animal feed, biofuel, and industrial applications worldwide.

Temperature 18–32°C (Ideal: 21–27°C)
Rainfall 500–800 mm per season
Soil Type Well-drained loamy soil
Soil pH 5.8 – 7.0
Sowing Season March – May / June – July
Maturity 60–100 days
Avg. Yield 4–8 tonnes/hectare
Seed Rate 20–25 kg/hectare
Vegetable Crop High Value Year Round Market Crop

🍅 Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

Tomato is a high-value commercial crop with wide culinary use, rich in vitamins C and A and lycopene antioxidants.

Temperature 18–27°C (Ideal: 20–25°C)
Rainfall 600–1200 mm per season
Soil Type Sandy loam to loam, well-drained
Soil pH 6.0 – 7.0
Sowing Season Year round (varies by region)
Maturity 60–85 days after transplanting
Avg. Yield 20–40 tonnes/hectare
Seed Rate 150–200 g/hectare
Root Crop Cool Season High Calorie Global Staple

🥔 Potato (Solanum tuberosum)

Potato is the world's fourth-largest food crop, valued for its versatility, nutrition, and adaptability to different climates.

Temperature 15–20°C (Ideal: 17–19°C)
Rainfall 500–750 mm per season
Soil Type Loose, well-drained sandy loam
Soil pH 5.2 – 6.4
Sowing Season October – December
Maturity 70–120 days
Avg. Yield 20–30 tonnes/hectare
Seed Rate 1500–2500 kg/hectare
